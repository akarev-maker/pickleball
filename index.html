Pickleball

First to 11, win by 2. Mind the kitchen.

Move: Arrow keys / WASD

Aim: mouse crosshair — shots fly toward it

Power: hold mouse button / Space to charge

Dink: hold Shift when hitting

Spin: hold E = topspin, Q = slice

Serve: Space  ·  Pause: Esc  ·  Mute: M

You swing automatically when the ball is in reach. Charged shots are faster but riskier.
